Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Why should I register my warranty?

Registering your warranty ensures that your product is covered for repairs, parts replacement, and service under the manufacturer's warranty terms. It also speeds up the claims process if you ever need support.

What documents do I need?

You will need your product's serial number (found on the device or packaging), your date of purchase, and a clear photo or scan of your purchase receipt.

How do I find my serial number?

Your serial number can be found on the label at the bottom or back of your BP Meter device. It usually starts with "SN-" followed by a series of numbers. You can also find it on the product packaging.

What happens after registration?

Once you submit the registration form, you will receive a confirmation email with your warranty details. Your warranty is then active and linked to the serial number of your product. Keep the confirmation email for your records.

How long does the warranty last?

All BP Meter products come with a standard 2-year manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase. Extended warranty options may be available depending on your model.
